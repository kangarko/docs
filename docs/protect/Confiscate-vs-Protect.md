# Confiscate vs Protect

![Confiscate vs Protect](/images/protect/CNszW7N.png)

**Confiscate** (2017): Takes items based on mostly hardcoded rules into a physical chest room. Not easily searchable, items can't be restored.

**Protect** (current): Stores inventory snapshots in SQLite/MySQL database with advanced filters. Searchable, restorable, fully customizable rules.

## Free license upgrade

Confiscate customers can upgrade to Protect for free. [See the link at the top of Protect's BuiltByBit page](https://builtbybit.com/resources/protect-prevent-dupe-exploits.44847/).

::: warning
Automatic migration between Confiscate and Protect is not possible due to vast structural differences.
:::
